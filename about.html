About SolarBright Energy

Leading the renewable energy revolution with innovative solar solutions since 2011

Our Story

Established in 2011, SolarBright Energy began with a clear vision: to make clean, renewable solar energy accessible to everyone. What started as a small team of passionate engineers has evolved into a leading solar provider serving residential and commercial clients nationwide.

Our founder, Dr. Emily Chen, combined her expertise in renewable energy systems with a commitment to environmental stewardship. Recognizing the urgent need for sustainable energy alternatives, she built SolarBright on the principles of innovation, quality, and customer empowerment.

2011 Company Founded
2014 NABCEP Certification
2017 Expanded to 10 States
2021 Carbon Neutral Operations
2024 50,000+ Systems Installed
Solar Installation Image

Our Mission

To empower homes and businesses with reliable, affordable solar energy solutions that reduce carbon footprints and energy costs. We deliver cutting-edge technology with exceptional service and support.

Our Vision

To accelerate the global transition to renewable energy by making solar power the primary choice for energy generation, creating a sustainable future for generations to come.
